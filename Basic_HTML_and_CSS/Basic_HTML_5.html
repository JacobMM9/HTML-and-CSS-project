What is BJJ

BJJ a.k.a. Brazillion Jiu Jitsu Was Originally Just Judo originating in Japan and was primarily throws and takedowns.
Its more common name at the time was Kano Jiu-Jitsu.It was then introdced to Brazil by a man named Mitsuyo Maeda to Carlos Gracie.
Gracie became a pupil and later developed his own style which morphed into a more pragmatic ground fighting focus with influence from capwada.
